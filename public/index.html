AEO Explained

Frequently asked questions.

New to AEO? These answers are taken directly from HubSpot's AEO product page — a good primer before the workshop.

What is AEO?
AEO stands for answer engine optimisation. It's the practice of improving how your brand appears in AI answers on platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and more. As more buyers turn to answer engines to research solutions, showing up accurately and prominently in those responses is becoming just as important as ranking in traditional search.

As an emerging discipline, AEO goes by many names, including AI search optimisation, AI SEO, generative engine optimisation (GEO), AI visibility optimisation, LLM optimisation (LLMO), and AI answer optimisation.
How does the HubSpot AEO tool work?
HubSpot's AEO tool gives you four things: a visibility score, prompt tracking, citation analysis, and prioritised recommendations.

The main dashboard shows your brand visibility score and how that score trends over time across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, with a competitive landscape and share of voice metrics.

Prompt tracking shows how visible your business is at the individual prompt level and the exact response each AI engine returned. You can filter by engine, buyer's journey phase, product/service relevance, and more.

Citation analysis breaks down which domains and content types are driving AI mentions for your brand and competitors.

Recommendations turn all of that data into prioritised actions — like creating a new blog post, updating an existing page, publishing a LinkedIn post, or reaching out to third-party sites.
What are prompts, and how many do I need?
Prompts are the questions or queries people ask AI search engines. In the AEO tool, you track specific prompts relevant to your business to see how often your brand and competitors appear in answers. Prompt tracking helps you monitor how often your brand is mentioned — and in what context — for questions that matter to your potential buyers.

Start with the free 25 prompts during the 28-day trial. Most teams expand their prompt set over time. You can purchase additional prompts at any time — there's no limit to how many you can add.
What is citation analysis?
Citation analysis shows you exactly which domains, pages, and content types are being referenced in AI answers. You can see how your citation rate compares to competitors over time, which source types (owned, competitor, third-party, social, affiliate, and more) and content types (blog, news, product, social, and more) are driving AI mentions, and which specific pages are being cited most often.

When you understand what's driving AI answers, you know exactly what to create, update, or optimise.
What do the key AEO terms mean?
  • Answer engine — An AI tool that responds to questions with direct answers rather than a list of links. ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity are answer engines.
  • Brand visibility score — How often your brand appears in AI answers across your tracked prompts, expressed as a percentage.
  • Share of voice — How often your brand is mentioned compared to competitors across the same prompts.
  • Sentiment analysis — Whether AI is describing your brand positively, negatively, or neutrally, measured on a scale from -100 to +100.
  • Competitor visibility — How often each tracked brand appears in AI-generated answers over time.
Do I need technical expertise or an existing HubSpot subscription?
No to both. HubSpot's AEO tool is built so any marketer can get started and find actionable insights quickly, without needing technical knowledge or AEO expertise.

AEO also tracks how your brand appears in AI answers regardless of where your website is hosted — you don't need to use HubSpot's CMS.
What's the difference between HubSpot AEO, the AEO Grader, and AI Search Sensor?
These tools serve different stages of the AEO journey:
  • AEO Grader — A free, one-time diagnostic. Enter a brand name and get a snapshot of your visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini: visibility score, sentiment, competitive positioning, and sample recommendations. A good starting point, but a single moment in time.
  • AI Search Sensor — A free, continuous tracker of industry-wide AEO shifts. Tracks AI visibility trends across major players and industries — but won't show your specific brand's visibility.
  • HubSpot AEO — Ongoing tracking and improvement. $50/month with no additional HubSpot subscription required. Includes prompt tracking, brand visibility and citation analysis, competitor share of voice, and prioritised recommendations.
  • AEO in Marketing Hub Pro/Enterprise — The same core tracking but with more prompts and smarter recommendations powered by your CRM data, with the ability to act on recommendations directly inside HubSpot's content tools.
