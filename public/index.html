Why This Matters Now

The discovery channel
has shifted.

ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity are now the first stop for buyers researching agencies. If you're not in the answer, you're not in the running.

🤖

Buyers Ask AI First

Before they ever visit your website or Google your name, potential clients are asking AI "who are the best B2B PR agencies in London?" — and getting a shortlist that doesn't include you.

📊

Your Competitors Are Already There

HubSpot's data shows AI is recommending brands right now. Every day without an AEO strategy is a day a competitor is being recommended to your next client.

⚡

The Window Is Still Open

AEO is nascent. The agencies who move now will lock in visibility advantage while others are still debating whether to care. This workshop is about moving first.

Workshop Agenda

What you'll walk away with.

01

What AEO Actually Is (and Isn't)

Cut through the hype. Understand the mechanics of how AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity surface brands — and what that means for your new business pipeline.

02

Your Agency's AI Visibility Score

Using HubSpot's AEO tool, we'll demonstrate live how to audit your brand's visibility — your score, your share of voice vs competitors, and your sentiment across AI engines.

03

Citation Analysis: Why AI Cites Your Competitors

Discover which domains and content types are driving AI mentions for agencies in your category — and exactly what you can do to get cited instead.

04

Prompt Strategy for Agencies

Learn which buyer queries to track, how to reverse-engineer the prompts your ideal clients are using, and how to build content that answers them.

05

Your 90-Day AEO Action Plan

Leave with a prioritised, practical playbook — not a vague framework. Real actions you can start on Monday: content to create, pages to fix, third-party sites to target.

06

The New Business Opportunity for Agencies

Beyond your own visibility — how to position AEO as a service for your clients. What to charge, how to pitch it, and why it's a retention play as much as an acquisition one.
