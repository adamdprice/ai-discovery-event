The shift

Search is no longer just Google

A growing share of your clients' buyers are starting their research in AI answer engines — and the brands that appear there aren't always the ones that rank in traditional search. AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) is the discipline of making sure yours does.

HubSpot has just launched dedicated AEO tools to track brand visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. We're bringing together a small group of agency leaders to explore what this means in practice — and what to do about it.
